Where Passion Meets Perfection

— Chef Ayaan Malik

Chef Ayaan Malik, the heart behind Forever Frost, is a renowned cake artist with over a decade of experience in crafting luxurious wedding confections. Born and raised in Hyderabad, Ayaan’s journey began in his family’s small bakery, where his love for delicate flavors and intricate detailing first took shape.

After honing his skills at international pastry institutes, he returned home with a mission — to create cakes that not only taste divine but also reflect the emotions of life’s most memorable moments. His signature style blends classic European techniques with subtle Indian inspirations, turning every creation into a piece of edible art.

For Chef Ayaan, baking is more than a profession — it’s poetry in sugar, frosting, and love.
